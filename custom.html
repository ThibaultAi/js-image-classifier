AI Demo
Inference Webcam Custom Classifier

This app is a custom classifier you can train.

Accept to use your webcam in your browser

Use common objects or face/body gestures to capture images for each of the four classes in order.
Each time you clickone of the Add buttons, one image is added to that class as a training example.
While you do this, the model continues to make predictions on webcam images coming in and shows the results in real-time.
The class no action represents no action
tested in Chrome
